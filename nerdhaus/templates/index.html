fab fun times7/6/2013

so as i continue on my magical journey to production quality flask, i discover my deep desire for a not dumb deployment solution. hence, fabric.

working with fabric, after some intial hiccups, is actually pretty delightful. it's a library created to facilitate interaction with remote systems via ssh. it picks up your 

.ssh/config
and uses it right out of the box, and comes equipped with a simple vocabulary for moving around the filesystem and working with files.

i intend to use this lib quite a bit, so more in depth accounts of my experience doing so will follow.
